Background

Malvadine is an Elite Warrior of the Mipedian Tribe.

Appearance

Malvadine resembles a tall humanoid lizard-dragon with mango orange skin and dark red belly and chest, squinting green apple eyes, long and sharp nails and horns of different sizes and thickness on his back, shoulders, knees and all over his long and strong tail. He wears a decorated scarlet red shoulder-chest metallic armour, gauntlets and knee pads.

In the Animation, starting on the M’arrillian Invasion, besides the overall reshaping, Malvadine’s colour scheme changes to shades of green, having now an olive green skin with a darker variation for his belly and chest, green apple horns and a bright, slightly glowing, green eyes.

Personality & Behaviour

As one of the few Mipedians without a natural Mugical aptitude, Malvadine devoted his life to honing his combat skills. His dedication paid off and he became one of the fiercest Warriors of Al’Mipedim, granting him status in the Mipedian Society, as well as the privilege of being one of the protectors of the Mipedian Royalty.

Combat

As a Mipedian Elite Warrior, Malvadine is always accompanying a Royal, Prince Mudeenu mostly, and, other than competently exercising his bodyguarding duties, he’s rarely seen in combat.

Abilities

The general idea of the Mipedians is hitting hard and fast and Malvadine exemplifies this very well. With his highly developed Invisibility skills, he’s a master of camouflage and his Surprise ability sided with Strike allows him to pack quite a punch in the very beginning of the battle.

Malvadine — The King’s Herald

Malvadine — The King's Herald
Malvadine — The King's Herald

We shall claim this city for Mipedim as we await King Theb-saar's return!

At some point after the Ascension of Kaizeph, briefly shown during the events of Elementary, Malvadine undergoes a noticeable transformation. Comes into play The King’s Herald.

Physically buffed and overly motivated, with a fancy and wide open shoulder-only armour, showing his now much longer and sharper horns, he, along with other Mipedians head to the City of the Elements to claim it for his Tribe while waiting for the return of the Last King of the Mipedians, Theb-sarr, from his ritualistic, self-imposed exile.

In Chaotic, like many of his fellow Mipedians, Malvadine now masters the Air Element. He also now counts with a Mugician Counter and becomes braver, faster and more resilient at expense of some of his power. But the most meaningful change is the removal of his Surprise ability to give room for the new one, Defender, which allows him to exchange positions with an adjacent Creature to fight on its place, even with a small boost on his Air Attacks, as long as the Player also controls a Mipedian Royal.

The Animation

In Chaotic, Malvadine was the very first Mipedian to appear in the show, impersonated by PeytonicMaster battling against Tom Majors as Maxxor in the Riverlands and later inside the Crystal Cave during the events of Unexpected.
