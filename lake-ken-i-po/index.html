Background

Lake Ken-I-Po is a region on the central-south part of the OverWorld, near the Runic Grove, formed by the freshwater from The Riverlands, on the north, after its revolving current calms down.

Its abundant water source seeps through the UnderWorld ceiling forming the region known as Everrain. And through its numerous underwater tunnels, invading UnderWorlders, like Nauthilax, often try to sneak in the OverWorld.

On the eastern part of Lake Ken-I-Po, atop of a steep hill, lies Najarin’s Castle, where Maxxor’s counsellor lives. Above its highest tower, Mugical emanations blends together the colourful Borealis resulting in very scenic nights.

It’s also there where the OverWorld Scout Mezzmarr, Antidaeon’s brother, makes his home.

But beneath its placid waters, Kaizeph, the City of Elements, remains hidden with all its mysteries, dormant until its Ascension during the unfinished apex of the Secrets of the Lost City.

Power

The Mugical energy constantly channelled floating above Najarin’s Castle prevents Mugics, played or not, to be targeted by other Mugics or innate abilities of the Creatures. Here, the most powerful of the engaged Creatures has the privilege of attacking first.

Number of Elements

The Ascension of Kaizeph is more than just the emergence of the City of Elements to the skies of Perim, it’s a ritual occurring in Lake Ken-I-Po.

And since the Lost city is the most probable source of the Four Elements themselves, here, none of Creatures can master a new Element nor lose any they may already have. The Creature who masters the most number of Elements has the privilege of attacking first here.

The Animation

The Lake Ken-I-Po is the Location that Klay programmed Major Tom’s scanner after he won his first match in Chaotic against Samuel “SamShady” Murakami, hoping to eliminate the competition.
