Background

Blazier is an OverWorld Scout and the cartographer of his Tribe.

Appearance

Blazier resembles a short, very skinny, and yet fatty, goblin-like with the most known of the Texan stereotypes: long, curved horns like those of the regional cattle, heavy accent and a big cowboy hat with goggles on them — although Blazier’s is more like a mix of cowboy’s and wizard’s o.O

He has a sunflower-gold skin, big light green eyes under heavy, hairless eyebrows, long pointy teeth, being the lower canines thicker and bigger, slipping out of his strong, square jaw, a mid-grey long goatee beard and a voluminous Mohawk hair hidden along with tiny ears under his hat and, on his hands, four thin fingers with moderately long and sharp nails. As for clothing, Blazier looks like a chilled out hitchhiker, wearing a little waistcoat, blue jeans-like trousers belted with a large cowboy buckle, a rope hanging around his waist and mountaineering boots. In his squared backpack, a roll of paper, allegedly a map, a bezel.

Personality & Behaviour

Blazier is a well-humored Creature who travels around all of Perim, mapping remote, unexplored and even on enemies’ territory regions as the OverWorld cartographer. Given his adventurous spirit and as such he’s not afraid of the unknown lying ahead, even if alone as he usually is. Often seen in his Mowercycle, he faces any sorts of obstacle, Creature or even the Location he’s at, on equal terms, and if the situations favour, even with some mockery.

During his expeditions, he usually has a nose for hidden secrets, always with that squinting eyes look, like if suspecting of something. While this innate curiosity helped him find the entrance to the Akarakk Cavern within the Crystal Cave — as mentioned during the events of A Rare Hazard — almost led him to death while exploring the ancient ruins of Gigantempopolis.

However, more than just a passion for being an explorer, to serve his Tribe with his skills, Blazier is ultimately looking for something. Something he’s been searching for his entire life. Something he didn’t realize he already found…

Even though Blazier is brave enough to venture himself in dangerous Locations, he’s not much of battling. But in time of need, mostly when his own survival is at stake, as a child of earth he empowers himself from the Element he has the affinity with. As a Mugic caster, Song of GeoNova and Geo Flourish are always a part of his Mugical Repertoire.

As knower of the lands, at expense of a Mugician Blazier is capable of manipulating a Locations Deck, reordering the first two Cards putting one at the top and one at the bottom at his will.

Citadel Cartographer

Archive

Blazier strives to be the first to discover new lands. History doesn't care about whoever comes second.

After years of exploration, gathering data of countless regions, uncovering all secrets he could find without ever finding what he utterly wanted most, Blazier realized the moment of revisiting the past had come. He needed to go deeper, thoroughly. Comes into play the much braver Citadel Cartographer.

With the new ability that allows him to expend his affinity to the Earth Element, the Citadel Cartographer allows for a sneak-peek at his map collection, deciding to stay and explore or move forward, off an already visited Location.
