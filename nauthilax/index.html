Background

Nauthilax is an UnderWorld Elementalist and a maritime spy to his Tribe

Appearance

Nauthilax resembles a humanoid monkfish. He has external gills located at the side of his head and glowing green eyes in the centre of big and deep dark circles. He has four pointy fins on his back and an inner body covered by a semi-transparent, gelatinous, slimy greenish material that stretches to form limbs and a seaweed-like goatee “beard”.

At the extremity of each of his upper limbs, Nauthilax has four long suckers. Down below, flat, non-slippery feet to help him adhere to surfaces like the muddy rocks of Everrain, his favourite Location.

In the Animation, starting on the M’arrillian Invasion, besides the overall reshaping, Nauthilax becomes discretely more colourful. His green body now has different tonalities, his gills and his “beard” becomes reddish, the iris of his eyes also changes to a light blue tone in a pinkish eyeball.

Personality & Behaviour

As one of the few UnderWorlders able to swim, Nauthilax is a valuable seafaring spy to Chaor and because of this he’s often on infiltration missions in the OverWorld, most of them through the several underwater tunnels of Lake Ken-I-Po. When not out on a mission, he’s usually around the Cordac Falls Plungepool along with Kerric, who patrols the region by air.

His loyalty is to his Tribe, but different from his fellow countrymen, he does not nourish any particular hate to the other main Tribes, even though he’s not exactly a fan of Mezzmarr, the OverWorld Scout who often frustrates his incursions.

Combat

In combat, Nauthilax is overconfident. Because of his stature, he’s a very brave fighter, however, for many times, the lack of battle wisdom gets the best out of him, having him often taking bad decisions, like standing still admiring the results of a single successful attack.

As a Water specialist, he can reshape all kinds of liquid at will, but as an UnderWorlder, even though not at his best, he also does Fire Attacks every once in a while.

Abilities

Because of his battle behaviour, Nauthilax is reckless causing him to harm himself each time he attacks.

Nauthilax — Tainted

When the M’arrillian Invasion effectively starts, Phelphor mind-controls the Guardians of the Deepmines, Nauthilax included.

Tainted by the M’arrillians’ powers, Nauthilax loses his only Elemental affinity and his Elementalist role in favour of becoming a Scout. Being controlled by superior minds does take away his Recklessness, but the mind effort required ends up replacing it with Exhaust, reducing all of his Disciplines, even if by a relatively marginal amount.

Becoming a M’arrillian Minion didn’t make Nauthilax smarter, however, it boosted all of his other Disciplines, in particular his Power, which was a perfect aide to his secondary ability that allows him to master a new Elemental Type whenever he wins a Power Challenge.

However, when commanded by a M’arrillian Chieftain, Creatures capable of brainwashing other Creatures, all of his abilities change once again. His Recklessness is restored so, no matter what it takes, he can focus on causing as much damage as possible, even with the aide of Power Outperform, that allows him to deal additional damage when whenever he wins a Power Challenge.

And all of this augmented firepower comes sided with a secondary ability to destroy a target Battlegear when Nauthilax causes damage equal to or greater than 25.

Nauthilax — Aquatic Spy
Hiding impatiently, just out of sight, Nauthilax soon learned that it's hard to let others do the hard work for you

With the defeat of Aa’une, most of the M’arrillians get back to the City of M’arr, behind the Doors of the Deepmines, and with this, all brainwashed and mind-controlled Creatures get back to normal.

And just like everyone learn with life lessons, so does Nauthilax. Comes into play the Aquatic Spy that, finally smarter, despite getting back to his reckless origins, is now a better Elementalist, mastering now two Elements, Water and Air, making the most out of powerful Attacks like Hail Storm.

The Animation

Outside Chaotic, Nauthilax was the very first villain to appear in the show, in Lake Ken-I-Po, during one of his incursions to the OverWorld, fighting against Mezzmarr and then Najarin, who came down from his Castle in aid of his friend in trouble.
