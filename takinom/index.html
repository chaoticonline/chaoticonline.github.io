Background

Takinom is an UnderWorld Commander who serves Chaor as one of his Counsellors, along with Agitos.

Appearance

Takinom resembles a tall dragon-winged humanoid with long black hair, red skin with yellow markings on her arms, legs and neck and sharp-clawed hands and feet, wearing a half-body iron chest plate.

In the Animation, starting with the M’arrillian Invasion, her skin changes to a yellowish tonality closer to her Card concept. Her body markings become dark green, the cheekbones more exposed, the hair more auburn-like and her thighs curvier.

During the apex of the arch, Takinom becomes Takinom, The Shadowknight. Her armour changes to a full body brownish-grey and black version decorated with reddish details with longer and sharper talons and spikes that leaves out only her face and wings.

Personality & Behaviour

Takinom is renowned not only for her unyielding nature and formidable battle performance but also for her diplomacy and manipulative skills. Although her temper flares often, differently from many UnderWorlders, she exercises great discipline and self-control.

As one of Chaor’s Counsellors, she spends much of her time in his Castle in UnderWorld City but she makes her home in The Twisted Palace, on a high cliff overlooking the Fear Valley.

Her loyalty to Chaor and to the UnderWorld is incomparable and makes her despise all rebels, in special Skithia, one of the faithful servants of Lord Van Bloot, and Krekk, whose loyalty splits between Chaor and Van Bloot as he sees fit depending on the situation. But this loyalty has a limit. A boundary that forces her to keep the bond she has with her archenemy Intress in secret, even from Chaor.

 
Combat

In combat, Takinom is a fierce warrior. Ruthless, relentless, restless, no sacrifice is too great for her if the outcome is the victory.

Her flying ability makes her swifter, giving her a savage and strategic advantage over her opponents and with her long and strong wings, she can protect her and even knock off surrounding them off with a flaming impact.

The Shadowknight
 
warlockss' DeviantArt

During the M’arrillian Invasion, Takinom becomes even more committed to her Tribe and comes into play the unique Shadowknight, changing not only her appearance but also her skills and abilities.

As Shadowknight, Takinom becomes slower, much of because of her new armour, and yet swifter. And after numerous battles won she got the expertise to strike only at the right moment and with full power.

She masters Fire and Air, greatly enhancing her combat proficiency, as she now can make the most out of several already trustable Fire attacks of her, but also combined devastator Attacks like Wall of Flames.

Takinom is born to command, but she was always a lone wolf. The Shadowknight on the other hand, is a strategist by nature, and understands that her UnderWorld companions are keys to the victory.

Because of this, her old ability Sacrifice is replaced by Strike, allowing her to hit once, hit hard and keep hitting again, thanks to a secondary ability that allows her, at expense of a Mugician, trigger the first ability once again during the Battle.
