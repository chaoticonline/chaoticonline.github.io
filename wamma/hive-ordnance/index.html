In the Animation, starting with the M’arrillian Invasion, Wamma has been reassigned to the ordnance branch of the Danian armed forces. With a big bag full of assorted Mugics, carrying them from the Hive to the battlefield, back and forth — with occasional stops along the way to pick up everything he dropped on the floor — comes into play, the Hive Ordnance.

The reassignment caught Wamma by surprise and certainly confused quite a bit this adorable and good-natured Danian, but after a few mistaken deliveries in the heat of the battlefield, as the Hive Ordnance he eventually adapted and became much smarter. Even though he remains a Mandiblor, instead of availing himself of the group work of the Hive, Wamma now also look after other low-ranked Danians fallen in battle and whenever they’re patched and sent back to the fight, he channels himself the energy of a Mugician, becoming able to help with the Mugics he carries around in his bag.

And by paying attention to how enemies initially react to a Danian Parasite when infected, Wamma eventually came up with a crazy idea of throwing his bag of Mugics on the enemies’ face, disturbing some of them long enough to prevent ostensive Battlegears, like the M’arrillians’ Kha’rall Shards, to properly work right away, effectively disarming these opponents for a little while and thus giving the Danians a small edge.
