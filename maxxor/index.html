Background

Maxxor is an OverWorld Hero and ruler of the OverWorld Tribe.

Even though it hasn’t been explicitly proven, Maxxor descends from Vlar, a thief once renegade by the OverWorld Tribe, as indicated at the very end of A Gigantic Mission. It’s also believed that Maxxor has at least some kinship degree with the rebellious Prantix and Accato and, in a savage branch of his genealogy, there’s Cromaxx, a nearly identical, yet stronger and almost barbaric version of him.

Appearance

Maxxor’s appearance resembles a tall, well-built humanoid with spiked black and white hair, hazel eyes, pointy ears and green skin with violet marks on his arms, legs and face and long and sharp claws. Maxxor wears a red tunic with a rustic belt with a big aquamarine coloured crystal and light brown leather elbow and knee pads.

In the Animation, starting on the M’arrillian Invasion, Maxxor’s overall shape becomes softer and curvier, accentuating his humanoid characteristics, and also with a less fluttering hair.

During the apex of the arch, Maxxor makes use of the Xerium Armor, a half-body golden armour with an independent lion-like helmet and ornamented gauntlets and cuisse, becoming Maxxor, Protector of Perim.

Personality & Behaviour

As the leader of the OverWorld Maxxor feels the weight of ensuring the safety of several other Creatures on his shoulders and because of this he always appears to be tense and stressed and sometimes even rude with Creatures (and people) he doesn’t even know well.

However, as shown in Castle Bodhran or Bust (Part 2), it’s all a matter of trust, as he’s always willing to sacrifice himself for the good of others.

His loyalty has been always and only to the OverWorld, but eventually he developed a bond with the protagonist of the show, Tom Majors, that strengthened more and more after the events of Maze of Menace.

He’s close friends to Intress, his right hand, Najarin, the High Muge and wise counsellor since the early days of Kiru City and, as very well evidenced during the last episode Son of the Spiritlands, Tangath Toborn. He was also a friend of Raznus, who later came to become the OverWorld Ambassador for the Danians, from his very youth. A relationship that was rudely broken when, after infected with a Danian Parasite, Raznus tried to assassinate him.

On the other hand, Maxxor has always been a fierce enemy of the UnderWorld, Chaor in particular who has always been intentioned to take Kiru City and rule all of Perim. An attitude that begun to change during the apex of the M’arrillian Invasion.

Combat

Maxxor is a powerful and skilful fighter. As a Tribal Leader, he’s always putting himself against the deadliest dangers without holding back and, in battle, he’s always with his army, on the front line, leading the charge.

Abilities

As an OverWorlder, Maxxor is supportive towards his squadmates, even healing them at expense of Mugicians.

Maxxor, Protector of Perim

Maxxor, Protector of Perim

During the M’arrillian Invasion, since Chaor is gone after the events of Underworld Overthrown and Odu-Bathax and Prince Mudeenu are conducting the military-side of the operation, with training, planning and coordination, Maxxor was up to the task of facing Aa’une, the M’arrillians’ Ruler, alone, by transporting himself to City of Ma’arr.

Even with Tartarek, the head of OverWorld’s spy network, providing an apparently solid infiltration plan, given the M’arrillians’ telepathy and mind-controlling abilities, Maxxor wisely thought for the best to have a secret backup plan of his own.

The outcome was Maxxor equipping himself with the powerful Xerium Armor, a unique battlegear that allowed Maxxor to drastically reduce any of Aa’une’s insanely powerful attacks and, thus, having a fighting chance.

Equipped with Xerium Armor, Maxxor then becomes “The Protector of Perim”, trading his supportive ability for even more power, becoming stronger and stronger each time he gets hit by an Attack.
