Chaotic Changelog rev. 3cf8783

Changelog

  rev. 3cf8783

 

Added

Structure-related changes

  • Applied Content File Componentization to the published Articles about Maxxor and Najarin

  • Added componentizable Content File, with their own Data Files and i18n entries, about the following Creatures’ variations: Maxxor, Protector of Perim; Najarin, Fluidmorphers’ Foe; Najarin, High Muge of the Lake; and Najarin, Younger

  • Added Content Files, Data Files and i18n entries for the following Pages as counterpart of the many changes made in the published Article about Najarin: Flame Breach and Fluid Flame (Attacks), Anthem of Stone, Battlesong of Renewal, Chorus of Cothica, Dissonance of Will, Ecstatic Fanfare, Howl of the Wind, Intertwined Melody and Song of Revival (Mugics) and Muge’s Tuningfork (Battlegear)

  • Added Content Files, Data Files and i18n entries for the following Pages as counterpart of the many changes made in the componentizable Article about Najarin, Fluidmorphers’ Foe: Heptagon Hail (Attack), Rao’Pa Sahkk Chimegrid (Location), Dissonance of Distraction, Requiem of Lost Minds and Requiem of Fear (Mugics) and Mipedian Balladeers’ Flute (Battlegear)

  • Added Content Files, Data Files and i18n entries for the following Pages as counterpart of the many changes made in the componentizable Article about Najarin, High Muge of the Lake: Glacial Balls (Attack), Fluid Fugue (Mugic), Arrthoa, Enre-hep, Kopond and Lore (Creatures)

  • Added Content Files, Data Files and i18n entries for the following Pages as counterpart of the many changes made in the componentizable Article about Najarin, Younger: Dibanni (Creature), Song of Deflection and Fighters’ Fanfare (Mugic)

  • Added Content Files, Data Files and i18n entries for the following Pages as counterpart of the references made in the Article about Wamma published in this revision: Ash Torrent and Magma Might (Attacks), Silchaw’s Mine (Location), Chorus of the Hive and Draining Dirge (Mugics) and Aszil, the Young Queen; Illexia, the Danian Queen; Wamma, Hive Ordnance; and Wamma, Flame Guard; (Creatures)

Hugo Templates-related changes
  • Added conditional statements to the Header Partial Template and each of the Cards Types META Tags Partial Templates to include the description and og:description tags to componentizable Articles that usually don’t have a Summary defined
Public Content-related changes

  • Added the Brazilian Voice Actor Mauro Castro as the voice of the right head of Cerbie

  • Added Brazilian Portuguese Voice Actors Dado Monteiro, in Episode #11, Season #01; and Ivo Roberto, Episodes #03 and #17, Season #01, for H’earring, even if they still require more thorough confirmation

  • Because the “official” (it still has some uncertainty u.u’) Brazilian Voice Actor for H’earring Ivo Roberto wasn’t associated to other Creature(s) yet, a new i18n entry for his profile in MyAnimeList has been added

  • Published Article about Wamma

Removed

Hugo Templates-related changes

  • Removed some unnecessary rules regarding Cards’ Images shadowing, merging it directly with those of the <img/> tag

  • Removed unnecessary characters from <meta> tags definitions

Public Content-related changes

  • Removed M’arrillians as enemies of Maxxor from his Data File, after all, the first appearance of Maxxor didn’t have any contact with the M’arrillians yet

  • Removed Xerium Armor as Favourite Battlegear of Maxxor from his Data File, after all, he started using this Battlegear only from the M’arrillian Invasion and onwards

  • Removed the Brazilian Voice Actor Luiz Antonio Lobue as the voice of Magmon in Episode #18, Season #01 because the only instance in which Magmon speaks in that Episode is distorted to represent Maxxor’s fear

CSS-related changes
  • Removed A LOT of sizing declarations of Creatures’ elemental affinity icons, grouping all of them in one single definition

Changed

Structure-related changes

  • With HTTP tunnelling used to debug the result of the OpenGraph’s tags implementation, Hugo Server’s start-up command-line became too long, so we added a Windows Batch file that needed to be ignored in .gitignore

  • Added the <!--more--> special markup to the published componentizable Articles about Bodal, Flamedrill Researcher and Takinom, the Shadowknight without which the visually hidden text of their Cards were being added as value for the description <meta/> tag

  • Changed i18n entries of the Brazilian Portuguese URLs of the componentized Articles about Bodal, Flamedrill Researcher and Takinom, the Shadowknight to follow the naming structure of the mains versions (i.e. prefixing them with /pt). While this change might not seem much for Creatures with their unique names, this reduces the chances of conflicts for other Card Types, especially in other languages

Hugo Templates-related changes
  • Made the <span> tag holding an observation about a Voice Actor a block level element making the text breaking into a new line allowing a few more characters to bet written if needed
Public Content-related changes

  • Returned the keywords to the published Article about Bodal that were wrongly moved to the componentized Article his variant, Bodal, Flamedrill Researcher

  • Renamed the Brazilian Portuguese version of Xerium Armor to Armadura de Xerium. Even though the Card name of this Battlegear doesn’t denote that the armor actually belonged to a Creature called Xerium, it sounds more lexically correct

  • With the change above, all occurrences of the old name has been update throughout the site

  • Renamed the Brazilian Portuguese translation of Tune of Xerium that appeared only in Episode #19, Season #02, to Melodia de Xerium, as it sounds better than the literal translation of tune

  • Renamed the Brazilian Portuguese translation of Tune Around that appeared only the last aired Episode to Melodia da Virada, as it sounds better than the literal translation of tune

  • Renamed the Brazilian Portuguese translation of the now fixed Tune of Turmoil to Melodia do Tumulto, as it sounds better than the literal translation of tune

  • Translated the Brazilian Portuguese version of Xerium Armor

  • With the inclusion of the Brazilian Voice Actors for H’earring, his Data File has been updated

  • With the new Brazilian Portuguese translation of Tune Around, the published Article about Heptadd of the same language has been adjusted to reflect the change

  • With the new name given to Wamma, Hive Ordnance, the corresponding keyword in Episode #11, Season #03 has been adjusted (Brazilian Portuguese only)

  • Updated Changelog Page

CSS-related changes

  • Changed root’s font-size units from rem to vh allowing the text to scale even if zooming in/out or if OS has a DPI higher than 100%, which is common with big screen monitors

  • Parallel to the change above, the images next to big chunks of text also needs to be rescaled and, to achieve that, the measuring unit of them has been changed from percentage to vw, the counterpart of the vh unit applied in the change above. With this, not only (some) images will scale better with the text but also will drastically reduce the blurriness of texts on them, like Cards’ description

  • Parallel to the change above, the measuring units of Creatures’ Disciplines and Elemental affinities sprite icons have been changed from px to vh/vw

  • Improved vertical alignment of Creatures’ Discipline sprite icons

  • Improved the positioning of Images Credits’ texts, especially on mobile versions

  • Improved the legibility of Links and Italic-styled texts with the main body of Danians, since the old colour wasn’t very distinguishable

  • Reduced the size of Creatures’ Elemental affinity icons, increasing their sharpness

  • Adjusted Quotes’ Authors positioning

  • Additionally to making the <span/> tag holding Voice Actors notes a block element (see above), the font size has been reduced and the text colour smoothed, showing slightly less importance if compared to the Voice Actors’ names

Fixed

Structure-related changes

  • Fixed a typo in one of the URLs in the published componentizable Article about Lava Pond, Milla’iin’s Foothold pointing to a non-existent Page while referring to the Riverlands’ Page (Brazilian Portuguese only)

  • Fixed some issues with the i18n file for Locations URLs in which, because of a typo in the identifiers of Riverlands, Erak’tabb’s Foothold and Riverlands, Abandoned Wastes, invalid URLs COULD be rendered. The issue wasn’t a real problem because these i18n entries are there more as a commodity since they represent the last, therefore non-anchored, Trail of the Breadcrumbs (Brazilian Portuguese only)

  • Fixed an issue with the i18n file for Locations Names in which, the because of a typo in the identifier about Kaizeph, City of the Elements, invalid URLs were rendered because the URL identifier, was overwriting this one with the name when dynamically adding anchors to texts (Brazilian Portuguese only)

  • Fixed Content File about Decomposition (Mugic) whose Data File was wrongly pointing to Canon of Casualty’s

Hugo Templates-related changes

  • Fixed an issue with Card Shortcode Partial Template for Locations in which some of the sections in the Bootstrap Grid System used for Cards’ Information were shorter than others with the same definition, in the same Page, almost like if its width was conditioned to the length of the Cards’ Names in the first cell-like structure

  • Fixed an issue caused, probably, when we reworked the directory structure a few revisions ago, that ended up removing the Summary of all Episodes Pages, leaving the corresponding Heading Titles alone, without content <_<

  • Fixed an issue with canonical URL that, locally, if Hugo Server is started bound to a specific domain, it remained set as localhost, preventing OpenGraph debug with HTTP tunnelling that exposes the local server to the Internet

Public Content-related changes

  • Fixed keyword about Tune of Turmoil in Episode #05, Season #02 that was wrongly pluralized

  • Fixed the Data File about Raimusa whose Big Picture’s path was wrongly pointing to Olkiex’s and, because we don’t have one for him (yet?), Raimusa’s images vanished

  • Fixed URL entry of Mowercycle in the Brazilian Portuguese Battlegears’ Sitemap File that was exactly like the English version’s

  • Fixed i18n entries about Mipedian Cactus and Telebracers URLs that were pointing to non-existent Pages

CSS-related changes
  • Fixed an issue in the box with the Voice Actor of a Creature (or with the placeholder text, in case none has been identified yet) that wasn’t vertically aligned with the label to its left

Image-related changes

  • Added Big Pictures for the following recently componentized Articles: Maxxor, Protector of Perim; Najarin, Fluidmorphers’ Foe; Najarin, High Muge of the Lake; and Najarin, Younger

  • Added the Big Picture of Song of Revival for the new Content Files added as references for the many changes made in the published Article about Najarin

  • Added Big Pictures of Enre-hep, Kopond and Lore for the new Content Files added as references for the many changes made in the componentizable Article about Najarin, High Muge of the Lake

  • Added the Big Picture of Song of Deflection for the new Content Files added as references for the many changes made in the componentizable Article about Najarin, Younger

  • Added the following Big Pictures referenced in the Article about Wamma published in this revision: Ash Torrent (Attack), Chorus of the Hive (Mugic) and Wamma, Hive Ordnance; Wamma, Flame Guard; and Aszil, the Young Queen (Creatures)

  • Changed the Big Picture of Notes of Neverwhere to one closer to the Concept Art of its Card

  • Updated the Site Brand Image align the letters H, A, O and T in an arc orientation with better curvature, by equally distributing their centres
