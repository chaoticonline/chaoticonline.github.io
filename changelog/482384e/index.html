Added

Structure-related changes

  • Added, as counterparts of the references made in the Article about the Forest of Life published in this revision, Content Files, Data Files and i18n entries for Slowsand (Attack), Reality Field Generator (Battlegear), Psimion, Abin-wor, Bivike; Grantkae, Mipedian General; Jorre, Mipedian Lieutenant; and Noaz, Mipedian Cavalerist (Creatures); and Tune of Xerium (Mugic)

  • Added componentizable Content Files, with their own Data Files and i18n entries to the following Pages added as references for the Article about the Forest of Life published in this revision: Ikkatosh, Guardian of Nature and Ikkatosh, the Aich King (Creatures); and Forest of Life, During Aichlyys, Mipedim Oasis, Aichlyys Festival and Rao’Pa Sahkk, the Ocean with no Water (Locations)

  • Added a new Page about Rao’Pa Sahkk to concentrate all variants of this Location through multiple componentizable Articles

Public Content-related changes

  • Added instructions for an inline CSS declaration in the Data File about Vinta so his Big Picture don’t become distorted

  • Published Article about the Forest of Life

Removed

Structure-related changes
  • With the change in the format of the Page about the Rao’Pa Sahkk Chimegrid (see below), the Content Files, Data File and i18n entries related to the old format have been removed
Public Content-related changes
  • Removed the FrontMatter Parameter listed from almost all componentizable Pages with each Changelog Revision as a counterpart of the changes made to the SEO Partial Template for Page Title

Changed

Structure-related changes
  • With the inclusion of the componentizable Article about Rao’Pa Sahkk, the Ocean with no Water, the format of the Article about Rao’Pa Sahkk Chimegrid added in previous revisions has been changed to be a componentizable Article itself as well
Hugo Templates-related changes

  • Reverted part of the simplification made to the SEO Partial Template for Page Title made in previous revisions because the headline Chaotic of the Day wasn’t appearing in the OpenGraph META Tags

  • Added an alternative for the full-formed Page Title without the headline to be used specifically in the <title> tag

  • Adjusted the SEO Partial Template for Articles and Listing Pages to use the raw Page Title, instead of the full Page Title built, because since the Page Title is used as Summary/Description when an Article has none, Pages without a proper Article end up with duplicated information between Page Title and Summary/Description

Public Content-related changes

  • With the change in the format of Rao’Pa Sahkk Chimegrid, the link to it on the published componentizable Article about Najarin, Fluidmorphers’ Foe has been updated

  • Updated Changelog Page

Fixed

Public Content-related changes

  • Fixed Page Title, Breadcrumbs’ Trails and Alternative Language of Changelog revision 6049e99 (Brazilian Portuguese only)

  • Fixed Page Summary of Changelog revision d75e5c1 (English only)

Image-related Changes

  • Replaced the Big Picture of Forest of Life (Location) with a version of better quality

  • Added Big Pictures for the componentized Articles recently added about Ikkatosh, Guardian of Nature; and Ikkatosh, the Aich King

  • Added Big Picture for the recently added Page about Psimion (Creature)

  • Added Card Images for the Forest of Life and Forest of Life, during Aichlyss, as a counterpart for the Article published in this revision

Miscellaneous changes

  • Updated all but Videos XML Sitemaps

  • Updated minified CSS and JS
