Background

Takinom is an UnderWorld Commander who serves Chaor as one of his Counselors, along with Agitos.

Appearance

Takinom’s appearance resembles a tall dragon-winged humanoid with long black hair, red skin with yellow markings on her arms, legs and neck and sharped clawed hands and feet wearing a half-body iron chest plate.

In the Animation, starting on the M’arrillians Invasion, her skin changes to a yellowish tonality closer to her Card’s concept. Her body markings become dark green. Her cheekbones are more exposed, her hair becomes more auburn-like and her thighs curvier.

During the apex of the arch, Takinom becomes Takinom, The Shadownknight. Her armor changes to a full body brownish-gray and black version decorated with reddish details with longer and sharper talons and spikes that leaves out only her face and wings.

Personality & Behaviour

Takinom is renowned not only for her unyielding nature and formidable battle performance but also for her diplomacy and manipulative skills. Although her temper flares often, differently of many UnderWorlders, she exercises great discipline and self-control.

As one of Chaor’s Counsellors, she spends much of her time in Chaor’s Castle in UnderWorld City but she makes her home in the Twisted Palace, on a high cliff overlooking the Fear Valley.

Her loyalty to Chaor and to the UnderWorld is incomparable and makes her despise all rebels, in special Skithia, one of the faithful servants of Lord Van Bloot, and Krekk, whose loyalty splits between Chaor and Van Bloot as he sees fit depending on the situation.

She also shares a bond with her archenemy Intress that was never revealed. A secret she keeps away even from Chaor.

Combat

In combat, Takinom is a fierce warrior. Ruthless, relentless, restless, no sacrifice is too great for her if the outcome is the victory.

Her flying ability makes her swifter, giving her a savage and strategic advantage over her opponents.

With her long and strong wings, she can protect herself and even retaliate groups of enemies at her surroundings, knocking them off with a flaming impact.

Abilities

OThe most suitable motto for Takinom is “survival of the fittest” as she can sacrifice her own UnderWorld squadmates to recover Energy.

Takinom, The Shadowknight

During the M’arrillians Invasion, Takinom becomes even more committed to her Tribe and comes into play the unique “Shadowknight”, changing not only her appearance but also her skills and abilities.

Takinom, the Shadowknight, becomes slower, much of because of her new armor, and yet swifter. And with numerous battles won comes the expertise to not be reckless and strike only in the right moment with full power.

She masters Fire and Air, greatly enhancing her combat proficiency, as she would make the most out of several already trustable Fire attacks of her, but also combined devastator steamy Attacks like Plasmarrow.

Takinom is born to command, but she was always a lone wolf. The Shadowknight, on the other hand, is a strategist by nature, and she understands that her fellow UnderWorld companions are the keys to a victory.

Because of this, her old ability Sacrifice is replaced by Strike, allowing her to hit once, hit hard and keep hitting always, thanks to a secondary ability that allows her to spend a Mugician to trigger the first ability once again during the Battle.
