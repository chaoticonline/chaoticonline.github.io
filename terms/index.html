Introduction

Chaotic Online is a personal project aiming to revitalize part of the Trading Cards Game “Chaotic” through (limited) remastering of the Animation episodes with the same name aired around 2006 and restorations / recreation of the original images previously publicly available in the Worldwide Web as well as original articles creatively written about Chaotic Universe after research and observation of the existent material.

We respect the nature of your personal information and precisely because of that they aren’t requested for your entertainment. However, in order to better know you and thus enhance the user experience throughout the site, some information is still collected.

How is this information collected?

During your visit, different information, like your IP address, if you’re visiting from a personal computer or from a smartphone, among others, are automatically collected by third-party services, requiring or not that one or more Cookies to be registered in your browser. This information serves both to generate detailed reports about our target audience and to connect you to products and services offered by advertisers based upon your interest.

Reports allow us to know, for example, which are the least accessed pages so we can investigate if they have any sort of problem — slowness, rendering problems that prevent them to be properly viewed and so on.

Your will and the control over the information collected

Since the site content is static, meaning that actions performed during your visit aren’t registered to the point of showing anything different than another visitor would be seeing, there are few ways of communication between each of the site’s pages. In order to not have you consenting with these Terms in every single page you visit in the same browsing session, we’ll register one single Cookie, individually, in each browser, of each devices you use to access the site, with a simple 1 (one) or 0 (zero) representing, respectively, that you agree or not.

Don’t worry! If the Cookie doesn’t exist we’ll ask you again whenever you change your device or clean up your browsers’ Cookies ;)

With this Cookie, you decide if these specific data will be collected and potentially used or not, because we’ll configure, as far as possible, the third-party services we use accordingly to your will, be it to disable them entirely while you’re visiting or to have them operating in an even more private way than usual. By blocking that this Cookie of ours to be created automatically means a not consent; after all, we won’t impose anything to you.

In our case, this usually means not authorizing the use of JavaScript in extensions like AdBlock, ScriptBlock (and others), when and if they’re enabled in your browser, or by preventing third-party Cookies to be used directly in your browsers’ settings, if they have such option

Note, however, that even though you can opt-out personal data gathering, Cookies may still be created by some third-party services, mainly, but not limited to, those of related to the site’s advertisers, for frequency capping, aggregated ad reporting, and to combat fraud and abuse.
