Introduction

Chaotic Online is a personal project aiming to revitalize part of the Trading Cards Game Chaotic through (limited) remastering of the Animation episodes with the same name aired around 2006, restorations/recreation of the original images publicly available in the Worldwide Web and original Articles creatively written about Chaotic Universe after research and observation of the existent material.

We respect the nature of your personal information and precisely because of that they aren’t requested for your entertainment. However, in order to better know you and thus enhance the user experience throughout the site, some information is still collected.

Cookie Policy

During your visit, different information, like your IP address, if you’re visiting from a personal computer or from a smartphone and many others, are collected serving both to generate detailed reports about our target audience and to connect you to products and services offered by advertisers based upon your interest.

How is this information collected?

This information is collected automatically by third-party services and, sometimes, this may require that one or more Cookies to be registered in your browser.

Your will and the control over the information collected

Because the site content is static, meaning that actions performed during your visit aren’t registered to the point of showing anything much different than another visitor would be seeing, there are few ways of communication between each of the site’s pages. In order to not have you consenting with these Terms in every single page visited in the same browsing session, we’ll register one single Cookie, individually, on all browsers, of all devices you use to access the site. This cookie holds a simple 1 (one) or 0 (zero), representing, respectively, if you agree or not.

Don’t worry! If the Cookie doesn’t exist because you’ve changed your device or cleaned all of your browsers’ personal data we’ll ask again ;)

With this Cookie, you decide if these specific data will be collected and potentially used or not, because we’ll configure, as far as possible, the third-party services we use accordingly to your will, be it to disable them entirely while you’re browsing or to have them operating in an even more private way than usual. By blocking that this Cookie of ours to be created automatically means a non consent, after all, we won’t impose anything to you just because you didn’t want to click a button.

In our case, this usually means not authorizing the use of JavaScript in extensions like AdBlock, ScriptBlock (and others), when and if they’re enabled in your browser, or by preventing third-party Cookies to be used directly in your browsers’ settings, if they have such option

Note, however, that even though you can opt-out personal data gathering, Cookies may still be created by some third-party services, mainly, but not limited to, those of related to the site’s advertisers, for frequency capping, aggregated ad reporting and to combat fraud and abuse.
