Background

Glacier Plains is a region on the north-easternmost part of the OverWorld.

It’s a harsh and forbidding region of eternal winter, with chilly winds and snowstorms, landscaped by high mountains covered with snow, freezing lakes and rivers and crystal-ice caves.

Still, it’s a quiet place, with deeply dark nights and colourful Borealis where Creatures like Blüggon, Aggroar and Arctus make their home.

In the coldest area of Glacier Plains is the Frozen Fire. A magnificent place, dutifully guarded by Aggroar, with a frozen sea of crystalline-blue flames, whose shards, when Mugically enhanced, serve as material for Ice Cloaks.

Beneath the apparent wasteland that the Glacier Plains seems to be, after thousands of battles over the millennia, lies the rumours of The Frozen, a secluded Tribe befriended of Blüggon only, that was never heard of.

Game

The cold environment of Glacier Plains seems to distune UnderWorld’s Mugics making them more expensive to be cast. Here, the most powerful of the engaged Creatures has the privilege of attacking first.

M’arrillian Melting Camp

In order to accomplish their goal of flooding all of Perim, making a big ocean out of it, the M’arrillians settled down a camp so they could build the heat cannon to melt the ice mountains.

The Mugical aura of the Glacier Plains almost feels this disturbance and unless a sacrifice is paid, as the form of Mugicians’ Counters, the trespassers will be severally penalized by having all of their Attributes cut-off by 50 each.

As a settlement for Fluidmorphers, Creatures that receives a Mugician Counter whenever they successfully hit their opponents with a Water Attack, they have the privilege to attack first.

M’arrillian Heat Canon

During the apex of the M’arrillian Invasion, the M’arrillians succeeds building their heat canon and start melting the mountains on Glacier Plains.

In the Game, the sacrifice of Tangath Toborn did when using the Symphony of Stasis Mugic to effectively disrupting the M’arrillians’ plan, is represented by a high Mugicians’ Counters sacrifice, allowing any Player, at any time, to win the whole Match.

The engaged Creature with more Mugical attunement, meaning the Creature with the most Mugicians’ Counters, have the privilege of attacking first here.

The Animation

The Glacier Plains is where the very first battle in Chaotic takes place, between Tom Majors, who had recently discovered that Chaotic was indeed a real place, as Maxxor, against Samuel “Sam” Murakami, as Takinom.

It’s also there where the M’arrillians settled down their camp to build the massive heat cannon for Aa’une’s ultimate plan of flooding all of Perim during the M’arrillian Invasion.
